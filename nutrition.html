Interactive Food Pyramid

Grains

Grains are a great source of energy and essential nutrients. Whole grains such as brown rice, oats, and whole wheat are rich in fiber and help in maintaining a healthy digestive system.

  • Whole Wheat Bread
  • Brown Rice
  • Oatmeal
  • Quinoa

Vegetables

Vegetables are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that are crucial for overall health. Aim to include a variety of vegetables in your diet to ensure you get a wide range of nutrients.

  • Spinach
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Bell Peppers

Fruits

Fruits provide essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They are also a great source of natural sugars, which can provide a quick energy boost. Aim to include a variety of fruits in your diet.

  • Apples
  • Bananas
  • Berries
  • Citrus Fruits

Proteins

Proteins are vital for building and repairing tissues. Choose lean protein sources such as chicken, fish, beans, and nuts to support muscle growth and overall health.

  • Chicken
  • Fish
  • Beans
  • Nuts

Dairy

Dairy products are an excellent source of calcium, which is essential for strong bones and teeth. Include low-fat or fat-free dairy options in your diet.

  • Milk
  • Yogurt
  • Cheese
  • Cottage Cheese

Fats

Healthy fats are essential for brain function and energy. Choose sources of unsaturated fats such as avocados, olive oil, and nuts. Limit saturated and trans fats.

  • Avocados
  • Olive Oil
  • Nuts
  • Seeds

Nutrition Tips

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Limit added sugars and salt in your diet.
  • Practice portion control to maintain a healthy weight.
  • Incorporate physical activity into your daily routine.
