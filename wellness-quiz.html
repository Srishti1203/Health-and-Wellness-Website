Wellness Quiz

Test your wellness knowledge with our quiz! Each question comes with a fun health fact.

1. How many hours of sleep do adults need on average?




Did you know? The longest recorded time without sleep is approximately 264 hours, or just over 11 days.

2. Which vitamin is known as the "sunshine vitamin"?




Fun fact: Around 50-90% of the vitamin D in your body is made by exposure to sunlight.

3. How much of the human body is water?




Water you thinking? About 71% of the Earth's surface is covered in water.

4. Which organ is primarily responsible for detoxifying the body?




Interesting fact: The liver can regenerate itself. As little as 25% of a liver can regrow into a whole liver.

5. Which nutrient is essential for muscle growth and repair?




Quick fact: The word “protein” comes from the Greek word “proteios,” meaning “of primary importance.”

6. What is the main function of the large intestine?




Did you know? The average person produces 1 to 1.5 liters of saliva each day.

7. Which mineral is crucial for the formation of bones and teeth?




Calcium fact: Over 99% of your body’s calcium is stored in your bones and teeth.

8. What is the function of antioxidants in the body?




Health fact: Dark chocolate contains high levels of antioxidants, which can help protect your cells.

9. Which hormone is known as the "stress hormone"?




Did you know? High levels of cortisol over a long period can lead to weight gain and high blood pressure.

10. How many minutes of physical activity are recommended per week for adults?




Fitness tip: Regular physical activity can help reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
